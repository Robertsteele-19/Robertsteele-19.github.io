About me

I am in the first year of my programming course learning to use C++. I also have hobbies such as cycling, boxing, drawing, anime and fighting games. Fighting games are my passion but I also want to give my all in programming over duration of my course and beyond.

Some of my favourite games of all time include Pokemon Soul Silver, The Legend of Zelda Breath of the Wild, Halo 3, Skyrim and Street Fighter V. I try to keep up to date with the latest trends in the gaming industry as well as current talking points.
